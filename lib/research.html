Research

  • Grammatical Usage of Vocabulary
    • Quasi-experimental design included specially designed free courses taught in the US for data-collection purposes, as well as data collected from intact classes in Korea
    • Literature review, assessment tools, and quasi-experimental contrasting of two teaching interventions were used to better understand and define the grammatical usage of vocabulary and the ways that students learn it

  • Creating a Computer Learner Corpus for German
    co-P.I.’s: Christina Frei, Ed Dixon
    • A corpus of computer-mediated communication was collected from 3 years of German courses at Penn
    • Data was organized and analyzed using TextStat concordancing software, and R

  • Heritage Language Learner Survey Project
    co-P.I.’s: Rachel Reynolds, Kathryn Howard
    • Designed a survey and surveyed over 400 students at Penn and Drexel
    • Calculated descriptive statistics and correlations using SPSS
